We allow filmmakers to raise money to create movies from the hits they already have, as well as find new audiences on the platform. This works through an pre-sales contract system. Audience members buy a stake in the film before it is produced and can act as normal investors or use their equity for views, special tickets and products.

Funding for Investors

The tokens used in the main funding are used to buy merch (t-shirts, toys, NFTs), or access (views, tickets, special events) to the IP.

Investors should be rewarded for providing seed investment by larger returns if the film is completed. This can be done through pre-tokens or NFTs.

DeFlix changes the funding dynamic to allow for riskier, creative content. The risk of investment is decentralised between many parties with no minimal requirements. The filmmakers' intellectual property is protected via smart contracts. This allows audiences and investors to directly fund the content they wish to see made without intermediaries.

There is a trust problem in film funding. The industry is very opaque as the fundamental investment is intellectual property which has a 0 marginal cost of duplicating.

There are many creative and competent filmmakers who are unable to secure funding since studios are unwilling to take risks. This problem is further compounded through Netflix's dominance and reliance on algorithmic content.

The process of raising funding heavily relies not only on filmmakers delivering, but also on the potential investors (namely large production companies and studios) not taking a creative idea and cutting out the originating director or screenwriter. This in turn creates very derivative and "safe content", restarting the cycle.

Deflix solves this through the use of smart contracts and blockchain technology. Smart contracts create a trustless environment without the need for large and expensive legal teams to enforce copyright. Blockchain provides the transparency ever lacking in the budgets.
